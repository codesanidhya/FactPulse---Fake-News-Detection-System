🧠 What is FactPulse?

FactPulse is an AI‑powered news intelligence platform that helps users see beyond headlines. Using sentiment analysis, fake‑news detection, and AI summarization, FactPulse transforms raw news into meaningful insights so users can quickly understand credibility, tone, and global impact.
